About THRISSUR TATTOO PARLOUR

Our focus has always been on clean and unique tattoos in a variety of styles. We create all of our own original artwork and make sure that each tattoo is sturdy and carefully applied. The goal of our artists is to translate your concepts into something original that will endure.
